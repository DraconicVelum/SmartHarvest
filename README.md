<p align="center">SmartHarvest enhances the vanilla Minecraft farming experience <br>
by introducing intelligent harvesting mechanics with automatic replanting, XP rewards, and tool-based bonuses.<br><br>
It makes farming faster, smoother, and more rewarding without breaking balance,<br>
making it perfect for survival servers, RPG setups, and economy-driven gameplay.<br><br>
Harvest smarter, not harder!</p>

---

### <p align="center">Auto Replanting</p>
<p align="center">Automatically replants crops after harvesting.<br>
Supports multiple crop types and is fully configurable.</p><br>

### <p align="center">XP System</p>
<p align="center">Earn experience from harvesting crops.<br>
Includes tool multipliers, drop chances, and optional orb spawning.</p><br>

### <p align="center">Tool Bonuses</p>
<p align="center">Different hoes provide unique multipliers and drop boosts.<br>
Encourages progression and better equipment usage.</p><br>

### <p align="center">AuraSkills Integration</p>
<p align="center">Seamlessly integrates with AuraSkills.<br>
Adds farming bonuses scaling with player level.</p><br>

### <p align="center">CoreProtect Support</p>
<p align="center">Hooks into CoreProtect for accurate crop tracking.<br>
Falls back to metadata system if unavailable.</p>

---

### <p align="center">🧩 Requirements</p>
<p align="center">Paper / Spigot / Bukkit / Folia (1.20+)</p>

---

### <p align="center">⚙️ Installation</p>

<p align="center">2. Restart your server.</p>
